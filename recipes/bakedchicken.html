Baked Chicken

Home

Engjoy this juicy baked chicken that can be the main course to any dinner. It is great for any night of the week, when you need something quick and easy. It only takes 30 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • salt
  • mustard
  • garlic seasoning
  • onions seasoning
  • pepper
  • paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Slice the breast in half
  3. Then, season in a bowl the chicken with all the ingredients.
  4. Let it sit for 10 minutes
  5. place a pan on the stove and add some oil oil and heat the pan until lightly smoke
  6. Place the chicken on the pan and turn down the heat to medium and set a timer for 5 minutes
  7. After 5 minutes flip the chicken over and set another timer for 5 minutes
  8. after 5 minutes has passed remove from the heat and put in the oven for 11 minutes
  9. after 11 minutes check internal temperature is 165 or above and remove from oven
  10. Remove chicken breast from pan and let it rest on a plate for five minutes
  11. after 5 minutes serve
