Amazing Pumpkin Recipe

Home

Engjoy this warm amazing pumkin pie that can warm up the hoildays. It makes a great dessert for the whole family to enjoy. It only takes 2 hours to make.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • pumpkin
  • sugar
  • eggs
  • cinnamon
  • salt
  • pie crust

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together
  2. Then, mix the wet ingredients together.
  3. Then mix the wet and the dry together
  4. Prepare the crust
  5. Pour the mixt into the pie crust
  6. And bake at 350 degrees
