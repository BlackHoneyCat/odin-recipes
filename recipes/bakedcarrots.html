Baked Carrots

Home

Enjoy this warm amazing baked carrots that can warm up the hoildays. Its a great add on to any main dish. Pack with lots of flavor. It only takes 40 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • carrots
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Wash and peel your carrots
  3. Then, toss them in a bowl with salt and pepper to taste
  4. Next line a pan with parchment paper and throw the carrots on there
  5. Next, place the pan with the prepared carrots into the oven for 40 minutes
  6. After 40 minutes pinch with a fork to check if tender and serve
